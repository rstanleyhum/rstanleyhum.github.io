Welcome

My name is Stanley Hum. I am an Associate Professor of Pediatrics at McGill University. My area of expertise is Clinical Informatics and Healthcare Datascience. I recently moved to Montreal in January 2020. Before that I worked as a pediatric critical care physician and Associate Professor of Pediatrics at Columbia University (from 2007-2019). Now I practice inpatient pediatrics. I have a Bachelors of Applied Science (Engineering Science) from University of Toronto and a Master of Arts in Biomedical Informatics from Columbia University. I am available for consulting in healthcare data analytics. In my free time, I enjoy playing tennis, going to restaurants, scifi TV and movies as well as tinkering with my computers.
